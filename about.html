About Me

I’m Ritvika Kedia — a marketer and operations-focused professional with a strong analytical background.

After spending several years working as a Data & Insights Analyst in the AI and TMT sectors, I’ve recently transitioned into a marketing and sales role at my family's real estate company in Hyderabad. This shift has given me the opportunity to build new skills in communication, customer engagement, and day-to-day business management while gradually reintroducing data-driven practices into our operations.

I previously worked at Zeki Research as a Data & Insights Analyst, where I specialized in SQL-driven analysis, data quality assurance, and insights reporting across AI, semiconductor, and technology domains. Before that, I worked at Analysys Mason, where I produced published research on consumer behavior and energy markets. My psychology background has always shaped how I approach work — combining human behavior insights with structured analysis to create meaningful, actionable outcomes. Now, in my current role, I oversee account management, customer documentation, site visit tracking, marketing activity coordination, and the sales team’s work. My goal is to blend my analytical experience with real-world marketing execution — from managing leads and improving customer experience to building the brand’s presence through structured campaigns and consistent communication.

I’m also in the process of learning more about marketing strategy and consumer psychology in real estate, while slowly bringing in data practices to improve tracking, reporting, and overall efficiency within the team.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about technology, human behavior, and creative expression. I enjoy exploring how data can influence business decisions in different industries, learning new tools, and working on personal projects that combine analysis, storytelling, and design.
